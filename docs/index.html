Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

GreenReader ("the App") is a putting green reading tool that uses your device's camera and motion sensors to provide an augmented-reality putting line overlay. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Data — The App accesses your device camera solely to display a live viewfinder with an AR overlay. Camera frames are processed on-device in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Motion Sensor Data — The App reads your device's accelerometer and gyroscope to detect the slope of the putting surface. This data is processed on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Advertising Data — The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device and usage information (such as advertising ID, device model, and general location) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We request non-personalized ads by default.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not collect location data (GPS coordinates).
  • We do not create user accounts or require registration.
  • We do not store photos, videos, or sensor recordings.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs beyond AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

All sensor and camera processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers. The only network requests made by the App are for loading advertisements via AdMob.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Choices

  • Camera & Motion Sensors — You can revoke camera or motion sensor permissions at any time in your device settings. The App will not function without these permissions.
  • Advertising — You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
